the fastest way to trade any solana token.

## how to buy

1. copy any token contract address
2. paste it in the bot chat
3. select your amount
4. done

trades execute in seconds.

## buy amounts

choose from preset amounts or enter custom:

- 0.1 sol
- 0.25 sol
- 0.5 sol
- 1 sol
- 2 sol
- custom (any amount)

## how to sell

from any token screen:

1. tap **sell**
2. choose percentage: 25%, 50%, 75%, or 100%
3. done

or sell from **positions** menu for all your holdings.

## token info

when you enter a token, you see:

- current price (usd and sol)
- market cap
- liquidity
- 24h volume
- price changes
- buy/sell counts
- token age

plus quick links to birdeye and dexscreener.

## settings

### slippage
how much price movement you accept:
- 5% - tight
- 10% - recommended
- 30% - for volatile tokens
- custom

### priority
how fast your trade processes:
- low - cheaper, slower
- medium - balanced
- high - fast
- turbo - fastest

## best prices

we use jupiter to find the best swap route across all solana dexes. you always get optimal pricing automatically.
