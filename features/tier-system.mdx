# tier system

hold our token to unlock better rates and higher limits.

## tiers overview

| tier | token holding | fee | key benefits |
|------|--------------|-----|--------------|
| free | 0 | 2.5% | basic access |
| bronze | 0.1% supply | 2.0% | auto-snipe, referral boost |
| silver | 0.25% supply | 1.5% | extended limits |
| gold | 0.5% supply | 1.0% | priority execution |
| diamond | 1% supply | 0.75% | unlimited everything |

## how it works

your tier is determined automatically by checking the token balance in your primary wallet.

no staking required. just hold the tokens and your tier updates.

## tier features

### free (2.5% fee)
- basic trading
- 20 wallets max
- manual sniping only
- 1 copy target

### bronze (2.0% fee)
- auto-snipe enabled
- 25 wallets, 3 snipes
- revenue share eligible
- custom referral codes

### silver (1.5% fee)
- 50 wallets
- 10 active snipes
- 5 copy targets

### gold (1.0% fee)
- 100 wallets
- 25 active snipes
- 10 copy targets
- priority support

### diamond (0.75% fee)
- 500 wallets max
- 100 active snipes
- 25 copy targets
- 50% off launcher
- vip support
